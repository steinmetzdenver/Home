About Us

The owner left the corporate pharmaceutical world and founded the Joshua Tree Ostrich Ranch in late 2014. That path was not direct one and took him first to an french language immersion school in France. Returning to the US, he toured the country in his truck camper, eventually settling in northeastern Arizona where he bought a log home and a few acres of land. Always a rural person at heart and a lover of the outdoors and animals, he set out building pens and acquiring ostriches. lthough he originally envisioned a ranch dedicated only to raising ostrich and selling ostrich eggs and meat, he saw the potential to expand into the tour business capitalizing on the heavy tourist traffic on the way to the nearby Grand Canyon West Skywalk. An extra home behind the main ranch house became a gift shop and visitors could enjoy feeding the adults and holding the chicks. Unfortunately, the tourist business is a fickle one and people were too hurried to get back to Las Vegas for a Ranch tour.
Fortunately, the gift shop was a fully functioning home and only needed furniture to become a guest house and the Joshua Tree Ostrich Ranch and Guest House was inaugurated in October 2015. The Guest House has been an outstanding success and has welcomed travelers from all over the world. The hospitality business has been a learning experience but a rewarding one. Meeting fascinating people and providing a homey spot away from home during their travels is something the author never anticipated when he set out to live as he always wanted just a year ago.
